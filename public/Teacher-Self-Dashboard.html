Voice Commands | आवाज़ आदेश
  • Say "Present" or "उपस्थित" - Mark present
  • Say "Absent" or "अनुपस्थित" - Mark absent
  • Say "Late" or "विलंब" - Mark late
  • Say "Half day" or "आधा दिन" - Mark half day
  • Say "Capture" or "फोटो" - Take photo
  • Say "Mark" or "जमा करें" - Submit attendance
